Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

What is the process for donating blood ?
The following is the procedure for donating blood.
1)Registration
2)Health History and mini-physical
3)The Donation
4)Refreshment and Recovery
5)Testing
What is the minimum age to donate blood?
The minimum age to donate is 17 years(in most states) , 16 years old with parent consent in some states.
After how many days we can again donate blood?
You can donate blood after 56 days of last doantion.
Do I need to pay any minimum amount for registering?
No money to be paid. Just register with us and you are good to go.
Is there any minimum weight condition?
Yes, you must weigh at least 110 pounds. Additional weight requirements apply for donors 18 years old and younger and all high school donors.
